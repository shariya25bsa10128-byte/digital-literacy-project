**Digital Literacy Infographic – Description**

This infographic provides an overview of digital literacy and its importance in the modern world. Digital literacy is defined as the ability to effectively, responsibly, and safely find, evaluate, use, and create information using digital technologies.

It highlights key components such as basic technical skills, evaluating the credibility of information, effective online communication, and ensuring digital safety and privacy.

The infographic also explains why digital literacy is essential, including improving academic performance, enhancing career opportunities, promoting responsible online behavior, protecting personal data, and developing critical thinking skills to identify misinformation.

Overall, it emphasizes the need to stay informed, safe, and responsible while using digital technologies.
