**Digital Literacy Infographic - description**

Digital literacy means the ability to use technology and the internet effectively.

Helps in education, communication, and job opportunities.

Includes skills like searching information and using tools such as Google Docs.

Promotes safe internet practices like protecting personal information.

Encourages responsible social media use.

Highlights risks such as cyberbullying, scams, and misinformation.

Helps people use digital technology in a smart and responsible way.
