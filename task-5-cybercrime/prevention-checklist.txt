Part B – Stay Safe Online Checklist (For College Students in India)

🔐 General Safety
- Never share OTPs, passwords, or UPI PIN with anyone.
- Verify links before clicking—avoid unknown or shortened URLs.
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on all important accounts.
- Do not trust unknown job/internship offers asking for fees or personal details.
- Keep apps and devices updated to avoid security vulnerabilities.
- Use strong, unique passwords for each account.

💸 UPI / Financial Safety (Important)
- Remember: UPI PIN is only for sending money, not receiving.
- Never approve “Collect Requests” from unknown people.
- Always check bank account balance—not screenshots—for confirmation.
- Avoid scanning QR codes sent by strangers.

🚨 Reporting Cybercrime
Report at: https://cybercrime.gov.in
Helpline: 📞 1930 (Immediate reporting for financial fraud)
