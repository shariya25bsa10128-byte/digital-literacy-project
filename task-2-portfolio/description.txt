Creating or updating accounts on platforms like GitHub, LinkedIn, and Stack Overflow helps build a strong online presence

On GitHub, users can create an account, upload projects (repositories), and update their profile with skills, bio, and contributions

LinkedIn is used to create a professional profile, add education, skills, certifications, and connect with others

Stack Overflow allows users to ask questions, answer queries, and build reputation in programming communities

Updating profiles regularly with new skills, projects, and achievements improves visibility and career opportunities

Using strong passwords and accurate information ensures account security and credibility

These platforms help students and professionals showcase their knowledge, learn from others, and grow in their careers
