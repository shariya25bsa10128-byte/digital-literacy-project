✔ Do’s
- Use respectful language in comments and posts
- Think before posting anything online
- Keep personal and professional accounts separate
- Verify information before sharing
- Maintain privacy settings on social media

❌ Don’ts
- Do not post offensive or abusive content
- Do not share fake news or rumors
- Do not reveal personal information publicly
- Do not argue aggressively online
- Do not post anything that can harm your reputation
